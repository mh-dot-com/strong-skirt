Strong Skirt, founded by Larry Harmon in 1998, is a fabricated metal skirting system designed specifically for manufactured homes. It's all 29-gauge steel — bottom track, flex mount, panels, and top track. And, Strong Skirt is FHA Compliant.

Strong. Beautiful.
  Experience the difference.

Strong Skirt is durable, able to resist weedeaters and even 100-mph winds. It's far superior to vinyl, yet still affordable — cheaper than Hardiplank or Smartpanel. With 40-year rated Valspar paint, Strong Skirt is convenient and low-maintenance. And, it's made of steel, the most sustainable material in the world.
